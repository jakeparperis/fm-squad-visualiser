Features

Squad:

• Uploading your squad automatically redirects you, but you can return to your squad at any time using the 2nd button above

• View your squad as lists of players by position (GK-DEF-MID-ATT) including their nationality, name and overall rating

• Click on a player to go to their page

Player:

• Contains information about the player's attributes

• Displays the player's key career and season stats

• Shows the player's overall by position

Squad Builder:

• This page allows you to plan your squad by combining the player and squad pages alongside a customisable formation

• How to use:

- Click on the third button above

- A list of the players in your squad is displayed on the left sorted by position and overall rating (scroll down to reach attackers)

- Clicking on a player will display their card in the middle of the page

- Drag and drop players into positions on the pitch

Disclaimer:

All overall ratings and related stats/attributes displayed are calculated by using your squad file with python code. This means that the results may vary slightly from the player's true abilities. If you do notice a pattern of inconsistent outputs, please don't hesitate to contact me on reddit.

How to start

(If you prefer a video tutorial: www.youtube.com/placeholder)

1. Download the custom view and place it in your views folder (Documents/SportsInteractive/FMXX/views): www.downloadlink.com/placeholder

2. Open FM and go to your Squad tab

3. Click on the view drop down, customise current view and import view - select the VisualiserData view

4. Select any player, and press CTRL+A, then CTRL+P, select html and save it wherever you want

5. Click on choose file on this page, find the html file in your computer and click the 'Upload and go to squad' button
